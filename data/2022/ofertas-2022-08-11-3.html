Nueva oferta

Senior Bioinformatics Scientist

In Puna Bio.

We are creating the future of biologicals, using resources from more than 3.5 billion years ago. We want to expand the frontier of biologicals, launching a next-generation technology.

We are looking for a professional to work on the development of our microbiological products for enhancing plant nutrition and/or tolerance to environmental stresses. The ideal candidate should have a background in bioinformatics mainly in metagenomic analysis of metagenomic microbiology diversity and microbial genomes.

We are looking for the leader of our bioinformatics team!

Their main responsibilites will be

- Creating and leading the bioinformatics´ team in microbiome and genomics space.

- Developing and leading execution of monthly, quarterly and yearly plan of bioinformatic research to meet strategic and operational objectives.

- Scheduling, supervising and coordinating the team activities and project management, ensuring that work meets established safety and quality standards, regulations and is timely delivered, establishing project goals and deliverables.

- Performing project specific duties, creating and managing documentation and reporting results.

- Serving as key client contact for assigned projects, establishing working relationships with internal and external client project teams and collaborators.

- Support technically focused communication and dissemination actions

Requirements

- PhD Degree (Bioinformatics, Genomics, Metagenomic)

- Post-doctoral experience (not excluded)

- Microbial Ecology and microbial engineering experience is desired

- Experience as team leader

- Experience in project management (more than 3 years)

- Communication and presentation skills

- Fluent written and speaking in English

- The ideal location is Tucuman or Buenos Aires. However, suitable candidates
who are currently in other regions, will be taken into account

We offer

- Challenging tasks, short decision-making processes and a high level of personal responsibility in a modern work environment with flexible work time models

- A company culture which promotes continuous learning and diversity.

- Growth oppotunities within the company and experiences in a global scale. (eg. projects and courses abroad)

We are proud to be an equal opportunity workplace and value diversity at our company. If you feel this job is for you, unlock your potential and apply to this career opportunity in our company.
