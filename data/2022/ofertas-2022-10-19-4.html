Nueva oferta

Decision Analytics Consultant/Bioinformatics

ZS  Buenos Aires, Provincia de Buenos Aires, Argentina Híbrido

Responsibilities:

Perform data management activities for scientific research data domains such as biomarker data, genomics and proteomics data, assay data (flow cytometry, immunohistochemistry, cytokines, etc), molecular data for synthesized molecules as well as proteins;

Create and maintain data catalogs, data dictionaries and metadata catalogs for scientific research data domains;

Create and curate taxonomies and ontologies for scientific data such as “cell lines”, “immune cell types”, “molecular entities”, “assays”, etc. Research and identify taxonomies and ontologies in the public domain as well as commercially available ones, ingest these ontologies into an enterprise data lake environment and then customize them for specific client purposes;

Establish and maintain “omics” libraries and advise clients and ZS teams on library governance;

Evaluate and assess strengths and weaknesses of “omics” and translational data sources via interrogation, and through potential data partners for advancing the data strategy for a given experiment, or for general use by a research or therapeutic area team;

Provide subject matter expertise to teams building bioinformatics platforms and research data lakes;

Evaluate and recommend bioinformatics tools and platforms for client projects;
Collaborate with clients and ZS teams to understand and frame scientific questions and required insights and translate into experimental design for bioinformatics studies and analyses;

Design and code analytical pipelines for various “omics” studies and experiments;
Apply machine learning and advanced data science methods and approaches to scientific analyses and bioinformatics projects;

Publish research papers and present at scientific conferences based on work performed;

Maintain a strong insight into the capabilities of potential external partners and competitors in bioinformatics, by assessing their informatics and data capabilities;

Synthesize and communicate results to clients and ZS teams through oral and written presentations;

Provide client and ZS teams project status updates;

Create project deliverables and implement solutions;

Guide and mentor Associates on teams.

Qualifications:

- MBA with a bachelor's (and often graduate) degree in a quantitative, analytical discipline, such as Operations Research, Applied Mathematics, Management Science, Data Science, Statistics , Engineering , Bioengineering , Biotechnology or Biology. In lieu of an MBA or PhD, 5-8 years of relevant work experience may substitute.

- Up to 3 years of post-MBA relevant work experience, and 3-5 years of pre-MBA relevant work experience, with evidence of strong analytic work (including use of advanced modeling techniques and tools such as R, SAS, Tableau, or VBA

- High motivation, good work ethic, maturity and personal initiative

- Aptitude for, and enjoyment of, leading and managing teams

- Effective oral and written communication skills that enable personal impact with senior-level decision makers

- Strong attention to detail, with a quality-focused mindset

- Analytic problem solving skills, with a creative and innovative outlook

- Client service orientation

ZS is a global consulting firm. Fluency in English and the local office language is required; additional fluency in Portuguese is desirable.

Candidates must possess or be able to obtain work authorization for their intended country of employment. An on-line application, including a full set of transcripts (official or unofficial), is required to be considered.
